  • Restaurants menus

    There is a multitude of restaurants, cafeterias, and food stands at EPFL, offering a wide variety of dishes. PocketCampus shows all the menus for the day and helps you choose your cafeteria for lunch. Liked your dish ? Don't forget to rate it to help your peers find the best place to go !

  • Transport

    There are more metro stops in your life than just EPFL and home, and managing them is not easy. PocketCampus features a smart transportation system that automatically picks the bus or metro station from your list of stations that is closest to you, so you can just take out your phone and see when you have to hop on the bus to get to any of your destinations.

  • IS-Academia

    What's my next class, where should I go? PocketCampus can easily tell you that, showing you your schedule and the location of the rooms on the map.

  • Map

    As a new student, but even after a few years, the EPFL campus feels like a giant maze. PocketCampus knows all about where each classroom and everyone’s office is and displays them on a detailed map, automatically selecting the right floor.

  • Print

    Print PDFs at EPFL directly from your iPhone in one tap! Configuring printers belongs to the past.

  • Directory

    Looking for someone? You can now find the phone number, office and picture of anyone on campus in instant.

  • News

    New startups, exciting researches and new nominations: keep an eye on everything that's happening at EPFL from your phone.

  • Moodle

    Coursework used to mean a heavy backpack. PocketCampus makes your coursework light and digital, reachable in a few clicks. You can log in to Moodle, download today’s lectures slides, and revise them while taking the metro back home.

  • Camipro

    The Camipro card is your EPFL identity card, and one of its uses is to pay for food, coffee, printing, or cookies. If you have enough money. You can use PocketCampus to easily check your Camipro balance. You can even have statistics on your average monthly expenses.

  • Events

    There is always activity at EPFL !
    PocketCampus gives you all the information your are looking for upcoming events at EPFL. Conferences, festivals, movies, there is something for everybody.

  • Menus

    Des dizaines de restaurants, caféterias et stands existent à l'EPFL. Il y en a pour tous les goûts ! Pour ne pas se perdre parmis toutes ces possibilités et trouver ce que vous aimez, vous pouvez compter sur PocketCampus, qui vous montrent en plus les notes attribuées à chaque plat par ceux qui les ont déjà testés.

  • Transports publics

    PocketCampus met à votre disposition un système d’affichage intelligent des horaires. En utilisant votre position, PocketCampus devine d'où vous voulez partir et vous indique en quelques secondes tous les prochains départs vers vos destinations habituelles. Métro, train, bateau, tous les moyens sont bons !

  • IS-Academia

    Quel est mon prochain cours, où dois-je aller ? PocketCampus peut facilement répondre à ces question en affichant ton horaire détaillé et la position des salles sur la carte.

  • Plan

    En tant que nouvel étudiant, et même après quelques années, le campus de l’EPFL peut se révéler être un véritable labyrinthe. Difficile parfois de trouver la salle que l’on cherche. PocketCampus se révèle très utile dans ce cas en vous affichant la position de la salle en question (ou la personne) sur un plan détaillé du campus. Impossible de se perdre !

  • Impression

    Imprimez des PDFs à l'EPFL directement depuis votre iPhone! Oubliez les configurations d'imprimantes, elles appartiennent au passé.

  • Moodle

    Les cours sont traditionnellement synonymes de papiers et de livres, lourds à porter et peu pratique à lire en déplacement.   • Annuaire

  • News

  • Camipro

Vous pouvez l’utiliser pour payer à peu près tout sur le campus : nourriture, boissons, impressions, etc. PocketCampus vous permet de vérifier facilement s’il vous reste assez d'argent sur votre carte. Mais vous pouvez également afficher l’historique de vos dernières transactions, et même des statistiques concernant vos dépenses mensuelles.

We are always thinking of ways to improve PocketCampus, to make your life easier. Below, you'll find some of our ideas and prototypes. Feel free to try them out and give us feedback.



  • FreeRoom

    Are you a student in need of a room to study? PocketCampus is here for you. The new FreeRoom functionality allows you to see what rooms are accessible when. Click here for information on how to get FreeRoom on your Android device.

  • Microphone

    When you're at a conference, passing around mics is silly, because you waste lots of time. But, you already have a mic with you, your smartphone. We're now experimenting with enabling PocketCampus to act as a microphone. Click here for information on how to get it on your Android device (coming soon).

  • No queue

    Nobody likes queues. At PocketCampus, we're trying to make queues a thing of the past. By using the latest Bluetooth technology, we are able to track how big a queue is, so you can make the best decision. Click here for information on how to get it on your Android device (coming soon).

A few numbers

PocketCampus has more than 4'500 users, of which 1'600 use it daily.


The PocketCampus codebase is made out of three parts:

  • Server
  • Android app
  • iOS app

    • The server is composed of 10'000 lines of codes, mainly written in Java.
    The Android app is also programmed in Java and contains about 18'000 lines of code.
    Finally, the iOS app is written in the Objective-C programming language and represents about 20'000 lines of code.
    These numbers exclude auto-generated code and third-party libraries.
