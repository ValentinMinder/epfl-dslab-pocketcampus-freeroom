The PocketCampus Event for EcoCloud's 2013 Annual Event

As per the PocketCampus proposal, this is the website that details how event organizers can upload the following information:

  • A list of participants' name, affiliation, areas of interest, email address, mugshot.

    PocketCampus already imported the data EcoCloud organizers provided to PocketCampus on May 24. You can find it here.

    The spreadsheet must be updated by the EcoCloud organizers with the new participants who registered after May 24th.

    For attendees outside EPFL, EcoCloud organizers must fill in the PictureURL column. For EPFL attendees, this information will be automatically filled in by PocketCampus. If the automated procedure fails (one can see this as a blank row in the PictureURL column), EcoCloud organizers should specify the missing information. Please do not fill the FullName, PC_ID, PC_CATEG, EPFL_SCIPER, userId, exchangeToken, and accessTo fields!

  • The detailed schedule.

    PocketCampus already imported the data EcoCloud organizers provided to PocketCampus after the first meeting. You can find it here.

    However, information such as the abstract of the talk, speaker biography, and, for non-EPFL speakers, the speaker's picture and link to homepage are missing. EcoCloud organizers need to update the spreadsheet. For a speaker's picture, please update the PictureURL field in this spreadsheet. Please do not fill the PC_ID, PC_CATEG, and SPEAKER_PC_ID fields!

  • A list of posters: title, authors, URL to a PDF version of the poster.

    PocketCampus already imported the data EcoCloud organizers provided to PocketCampus after the first meeting. You can find it here.

    However, the link to the PDF version of the poster is missing, so EcoCloud organizers need to update the spreadsheet once the missing information becomes available. Please do not fill the Author1, Author2, Author3, ThumbnailLink, PC_CATEG, Poster_Author1_PC_ID, Poster_Author2_PC_ID, Poster_Author3_PC_ID, AUTHOR_PC_ID, and LAB_PC_ID fields!

  • A list of EcoCloud labs and corresponding details.

    For each lab, EcoCloud organizers must supply: the name of the lab, its acronym, a link to the lab's logo, the name of the lab's director, and a link to the lab's homepage by editing this spreadsheet. Please do not fill the PC_ID, PC_CATEG, DIRECTOR_PC_ID, ATTENDING_MEMBERS_IDS, and ATTENDING_MEMBERS_NAMES fields!

  • A list of affiliates and corresponding details.

    EcoCloud organizers need to update the spreadsheet. Please do not fill the Representatives_Names, PC_ID, PC_CATEG, and Representatives_IDs fields!

  • Venue information.

    EcoCloud organizers need to update the spreadsheet. Please do not fill the PC_ID and PC_CATEG fields!
