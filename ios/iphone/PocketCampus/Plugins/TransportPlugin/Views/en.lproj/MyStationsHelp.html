The "My stations" section has two purposes: let you manage your stations and select the departure point.

Manage your stations

You can add up to 10 stations by tapping the + button. Stations can be reordered or deleted by tapping the "Edit" button.

Select the departure point

Two modes are available:
  • Automatic: the nearest station in the list will automatically be selected as departure.
  • Manual: you manually select a departure station in the list.
