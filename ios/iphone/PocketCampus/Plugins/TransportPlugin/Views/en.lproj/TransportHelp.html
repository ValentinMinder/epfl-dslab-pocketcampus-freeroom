Transport schedules in PocketCampus are based on a very simple idea that shows you next departures very quickly.
  • You first define the transport stations you often pass by. For example: EPFL and the closest station to your home. By default, EPFL and Lausanne-Flon are proposed.
  • PocketCampus will then automatically select every time the nearest station as departure and show the next departures to all other defined stations.
    You can also choose the departure station manually among your stations by touching the bookmarks button.

Manage your stations

To manage your stations, tap the bookmarks button. From there, you can add, remove and reorder your stations.

Location error

This message is displayed if your current location cannot be determined or if you have denied its access.
You can correct this behavior by enabling « EPFL » to have access to your location in the Settings of your iPhone, under Location services (Privacy in iOS6).
Your location is never sent to a server and is only used to determine the nearest station among your stations. Once this task done, the location service is disabled to preserve your battery life.
