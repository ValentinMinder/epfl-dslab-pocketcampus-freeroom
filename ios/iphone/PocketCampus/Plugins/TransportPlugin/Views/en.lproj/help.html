Transport schedules in PocketCampus are based on a very simple idea that helps you see next departures very quickly.
  • You define a list of favorite transport stations. For example EPFL and the closest station to your home. By default, EPFL and Lausanne-Flon are proposed.
  • PocketCampus will then automatically select every time the nearest favorite station as departure and show the next departures to all your other favorite stations.
    You can also choose the departure station manually among your favorite stations.

Manage your favorite stations

To manage your favorite stations, touch the blue bookmarks button. From there, you can add, remove and reorder stations by touching the Edit button.
