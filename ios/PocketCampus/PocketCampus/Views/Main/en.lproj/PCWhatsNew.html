• EPFL email
    PocketCampus can automatically setup your EPFL email adress in the iOS Mail app.
    Go to the PocketCampus settings from the main menu, then select EPFL email.
  • EPFL VPN
    PocketCampus can automatically setup EPFL VPN. The VPN allows you to access EPFL's internal network even when you are not on the campus.
    Go to the PocketCampus settings from the main menu, then select EPFL VPN.
