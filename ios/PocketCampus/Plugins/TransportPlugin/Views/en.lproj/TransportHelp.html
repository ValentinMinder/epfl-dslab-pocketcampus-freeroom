Transport schedules in PocketCampus are based on a very simple idea, that allows you to see next departures very quickly.
  • You first define the transport stations you often pass by. For example: EPFL and the closest station to your home. By default, EPFL and Lausanne-Flon (M1) are proposed.
  • PocketCampus will then automatically select every time the nearest station as departure and show the next departures to all your other stations.
    You can also choose the departure station manually by tapping the "From:" in the top bar.

Manage your stations

To manage your stations, tap the
"list button" (bottom-right corner).
There, you can add, remove and reorder your stations.

Location error

If your current location cannot be determined or if you have denied its access, the nearest departure station feature is not available.
You can enable this feature by authorizing "EPFL" to access your location in Settings > Privacy > Location.
Your location is never sent to a server and is only used to determine the nearest station among your stations.
